About DJ knack

DJ knack, also known as the King of Bollywood, is a dynamic and electrifying DJ who has captivated audiences worldwide. With his exceptional ability to blend Bollywood, commercial, and electronic beats, he has performed in cities like South Africa, Bali, Dubai, Thailand, and Baku, delivering unforgettable sets at top clubs, weddings, and corporate events. His signature Bolly Tech remixes, including the highly anticipated Sajni Re, have set dance floors ablaze. A headliner at major festivals and a crowd favorite at premier venues, DJ knack’s high-energy performances and seamless audio-visual sets solidify his status as a true powerhouse in the DJing world.

When and how did you get into DJing?

I got into DJing in 2004, driven by my passion for music and the desire to create unforgettable experiences on the dance floor.

Do you produce your own music, or remix existing tracks?

Yes, I produce my own music and occasionally remix existing tracks.

What’s something unique about you that sets you apart from other DJs?

What sets me apart is my ability to blend Bollywood, commercial, and electronic beats into electrifying Bolly Tech remixes, creating high-energy performances that captivate audiences worldwide.

What’s your go-to track to get the crowd hyped?

My go-to track to get the crowd hyped is one of my signature Bolly Tech remixes, blending Bollywood melodies with electronic beats, delivering high-energy performances that keep the dance floor electrified.

What cities and countries have you performed in so far?

I’ve had the pleasure of performing internationally in South Africa, Baku (Azerbaijan), Bali (Indonesia), Thailand (Bangkok), and Dubai, as well as in major cities all across India.

Describe your sound in 3 words?

My sound is energetic, cinematic, and infectious, blending high-energy beats with immersive storytelling to create unforgettable experiences.



Favorite clubs you would love to play or have played?

There are many clubs around the world that are my favorites, each offering a unique vibe and energy that make them special to perform at.
